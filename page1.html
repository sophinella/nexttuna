The mission

next tuna will be the first company to close the reproductive cycle of the Atlantic Bluefin Tuna (ABT) in aquaculture in order to create the only sustainable source of ABT, a highly important and very healthy source of food to hundreds of million.

The issue

The EUR 36 billion global tuna market is fully based on wild catch of tuna. To date, tuna cannot be reproduced in aqua farms at large scale. This unsustainable approach endangers wild tuna species. 

The solution

next tuna will plan and build the first land-based, tuna specific aqua farm and successfully breed tuna in captivity. We focus on Atlantic Bluefin Tuna, as the most valuable tuna species. This will open the path to the sustainable supply of healthy Atlantic Bluefin Tuna to meet the world’s growing demand.
